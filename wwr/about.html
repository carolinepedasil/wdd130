History

Established in 1995, Whitewater Rafting Adventures has guided thousands of adventurers through some of the most exciting rivers in the region. We are proud to be leaders in safety and eco-friendly tourism.

Over the years, we have expanded our services, offering thrilling experiences while maintaining our commitment to sustainability and safety.
